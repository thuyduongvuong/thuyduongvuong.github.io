I am a 4th year PhD student in the Computer Science Theory Group at Stanford University advised by Nima Anari and Moses Charikar. I use geometry of polynomial and theory of high dimensional expanders in designing sampling, counting and optimization algorithms, particularly in the analysis of Markov chain.
I did my undergraduate at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), in Mathematics and Computer Science.
My research is supported by a Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship.
